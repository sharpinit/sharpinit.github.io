About AirClass

AirClass was developed with remote classrooms in mind with a mission to make better instructors. AirClass was a virtual classroom where corporate trainers could train remote teams. With the assistance of artificial intelligence an instructor could know if the audience was declining in engagement and if the topic needed to be changed or even repeated.

Unfortunetly, Lenovo made the decision to table AirClass and put their resources and attention into Airstack.

The Challenge

Increase retention.


Understand the market and capture their needs.

My Role

I was brought on to lead AirClass' User Experience, primarily working with the product manager and helping with any new feature requests and understanding the needs and problems users were wanting to solve.

AirClass was already running and had been originally designed by another member on the UX team I worked with.
