HealthEquity | UX Design

HealthEquity I was brought on to primarily help with the employer portal. It was an application they built for employers to use when they needed to add new employees to their HSA/FSA benefits. It had obviously been neglected and was the primary painpoint for most employers trying to do their daying tasks./p>

I also worked with the mobile team to create a better experience based on the data receieved from Google Analytics as well as usability testing.

I also took on an important task of informing the user of some important legal changes. I really wanted to capture the importance of the message and get that to the user as quick as possible, knowing that most users skip or skim legal documents on their devices.
