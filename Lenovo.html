About Airstack

Airstack is an application that allows business owners, managers, and teams a place to collaborate and organize their apps, docs, and tools.

The Challenge

High-level goals:


Create an application that could be bundled with the hardware sold to small and medium-sized businesses.


Create a conferencing service for those small-medium businesses.


Business Question: Could we reuse the conferencing technology for other products when necessary? (TBD)

My Role

I led the User Experience and Product Design for the conferencing feature and led the User Interface and Visual Design for the mobile application of Airstack. Airstack has a few collaborators working side by side with me and it was vital we collaborated and frequently discussed changes to stay consistent.

The Process

Every product starts with a problem to solve for a user. We, as a team had a hypothesis of what those problems were, especially with prior knowledge coming from a legacy product AirClass (RIP). These hypotheses, however, were not concrete until we got out and started researching and interviewing users.


After analyzing the beginnings of the data I created a simple ‘paper’ prototype. This included 30 pages of what could be the minimum features necessary to have a successful experience for users.
