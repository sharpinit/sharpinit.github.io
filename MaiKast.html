MaiKast | Logo & Visual Design

MaiKast is an app created by individuals who love to build things they need or use. MaiKast was at the start of life when they brought me into the group. The name was in flux and a lot of the user interface was still being decided on. I assisted getting MaiKast a new identity, graphics for the various app stores, and the interface for the iOS app.
