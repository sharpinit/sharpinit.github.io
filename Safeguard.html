Dell One Identity Safeguard | Interaction & Visual Design

Dell One Identity Safeguard, or Safeguard, is an enterprise solution that securely stores and rotates the passwords for small, medium, and large businesses. Allowing any size customer the ability to secure their passwords meant that the users workflow could have many variables and solutions that needed to be played out and understood before implementation. Safeguard also brought the challenge of being an on-premise appliance that could potentially never talk to the outside world. With all of the complexities behind Safeguard we were able to get out our first version released and started working on vNext. This project includes user experience, visual, and branding design where I got to work with another UX designer (locally), and collaborated with the broader Dell UX team.
