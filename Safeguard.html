Dell One Identity Safeguard | Interaction & Visual Design

Dell One Identity Safeguard is an enterprise appliance that securily stores and changes the passwords for small, medium, and large business. Being that we wanted to allow many customer types the oppurtunity to secure their passwords there is a lot of complexity in the interface.

Safeguard's experience starts at the box and we recognized that early on that it needed to be enhanced and updated. We initially were told that the poster was folded, designed, and placed inside of a smaller box that the appliance sat on. We then took a prototype to a few different database center individuals to see how they actually experienced the receiving of a box. We then found that they small box of contents and poster were usually thrown aside leaving the user with many questions on how to set the appliance up. We took this data back and put the poster on top of the appliance, being the first thing they got to see after openeing the box, and designed the poster to be easier to read and highlighted the most important information on the front.
